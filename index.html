Transformer-based Machine Translation between Prescription and Pharmacy directions
Aug 2019 - Current

Electronic Transcription of Prescriptions (ETP) in health care has been suggested to have positive impact for prescribing process in safety, quality, efficiency for both prescribers and pharmacists. We are trying to use Transformer-based Machine Translation model to automatically transcripe prescription to pharmacy instructions.

1️⃣Solved model Domain Shift on health care using pretrained embedding from MIMICIII dataset. 2️⃣Augmented model with External Information like Drug, Strength, etc. 3️⃣Adding Lexical, Phrase noise to text to increase model Robustness. 4️⃣Applied Ensemble Learning and Numerical Checking to improve accuracy and avoid fictitious generations. 5️⃣Designed Rule-based Pattern Recognition to do Normalization and Simplification based on Pharmacits expertise.

How about Avengers: Endgame? Movie revenue prediction with hierarchical model. [Lecture Porject Report]
Jan 2019 - Apr 2019

With zero information of movie revenue before release, Is that possible to predict Movie revenue in cold start scenario?

Movie revenue was found followed two Gaussion Distrubution: High revenue and Low revenue. 1️⃣Based on obeservation, we trained High-Low binary Random Forest Classifier based on labels extracted from Gaussion Mixture Model (GMM) clustering. 2️⃣After classification, we trained movie Gradient Boosting Regression (GBR performed best) separately and using Back-off strategy to solving cold start problem. 3️⃣We also tested generalization of our classification + regression model on Europe Soccer Players.

Neural Network Models to Identify Medication Relations from Clinical Narratives.[4]
Nov 2018 - Mar 2019

Identifying medication relations between drugs and associated attributes automatically from clinical narratives can help develop advanced tools for decision support. We investigate the strengths of neural network models to identify eight medication relations. We find that relation extraction is sensitive to complexity of data patterns as well as model capacity. Our results show that Bi-LSTM models achieved an overall F1 score of 0.892 on eight tasks, outperforming SVM and CNN models.

1️⃣Trained special domain word-embedding using word2vec on MIMICIII clinical notes dataset. 2️⃣ Feature engineering including part-of-speech tag, named-entities-recognition tag, pre-trained token embedding and bi-direction relative position of target entities pair. 3️⃣ Developed Bi-LSTM models to extract 8 relations between drug names and adverse events associated concepts with F1 0.892.


Baby blues: Analyzing Facebook and health forums on Pregnancy[2]
Aug 2018 - Sep 2018

In the study, we systematically analyzed questions posted on pregnancy forums by young mothers and contrasted it to a unique dataset of Facebook posts by expecting adolescent women. Distribution of themes across these platforms showed significant differences on roles played by the two platforms. We concluded that Facebook is chosen as a self-expression place to seek emotional support while health forum served as professional information provider.

PageRank++: European Soccer Team Ranking Prediction[1]
Jan 2018 - Apr 2018

Prediction of soccer competition results is always an atractive topic. However, traditional ranking method, Network of Networks (NoN), can only capture the similarity between players or teams rather than the win-lose relationship. Broadly used method Deep Neural Network (DNN) cannot converge within low-resource dataset. In this work, we proposed a graph-based method PageRank++ to predict the rank of teams in a league. Our algorithm converges faster without using large dataset compared with DNN and higher accuracy compared with baselines, Naive Rank and Naive PageRank.
